About Our Compnay

Who we are?

We are “ALEX GRUP OIL” S.R.L, and we deal with the processing, drying, and conditioning of cereals, oilseeds, the manufacture of oils and fats, as well as the wholesale of products, seeds, and oils. We are placed in the city of Straseni. We are two brothers, who have gained experience during several years of work abroad, thus we felt the desire and the need to achieve something specific and useful to people at Home, in Moldova. The courage, the confidence in good results and the experience in the field have helped us to overcome any obstacles. In 2020, we installed a modern line of processing, drying, and conditioning of cereals and oilseeds. In 2021, we started direct production and went on the market with ROYAL Seeds products – a range of over 10 natural and quality products – sets of seeds, nuts, and dried fruit – which can satisfy the tastes and needs of many categories of consumers. The final product is obtained as a result of the operation of a series of high-performance equipment and machinery, which you can see on the Photo Gallery page, and by the employees of this company, who perform their job with love and dedication.

We are in the process of implementing HACCP and ISO standards.

We are making considerable efforts to make continuous progress. The company’s goal is to develop and enrich its product range, including foods and new food mixes, to offer as many consumers as possible the opportunity to benefit from healthy and tasty products. In addition, we are absolutely open to cooperation, in order to export our products abroad. With the completion of the ISO implementation process, we intend to expand our sales market in Scandinavian countries, such as Denmark, Sweden, Norway, etc.

About Royal Seeds

“Alex Group” SRL is the company that has created the Royal Seeds brand. The Company is engaged in the processing, drying, and conditioning of grains, and oilseeds; in the production of oils and fats, as well as in the wholesaling of its products, seeds, and oils. Royal Seeds products can be found both in stores in the Republic of Moldova and in numerous commercial networks in Europe.
